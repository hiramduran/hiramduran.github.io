EUROPE'S TOP FIVE LEAGUES 2017-2018 STATS

ENGLAND

English Premier League

Top Goal Scorer: Mohamed Salah Goals: 32 Team: Liverpool

Top Assist Maker: Kevin De Bruyne Assists: 16 Team: Manchester City

Most Clean Sheets: David de Gea Clean Sheets: 19 Team: Manchester United

The English Premier League, often referred to as, "the prem," was formed on February 20, 1992 succeeding the established Football League First Division, 1888. With a monstrous TV deal, a league known for it's brutal physicality, soon took hold of club football's international viewership thus becoming the most lucrative league in the world. The league's most decorated club is Manchester United winning a total of 13 league titles; all of which came under legendary manager Sir Alex Ferguson. Currently, 2017/2018 champions Manchester City F.C. seek to defend their title from Liverpool F.C. and Chelsea F.C.

FRANCE

France Ligue 1

Top Goal Scorer: Ederson Cavani Goals: 28 Team: Paris Saint-Germain F.C.

Top Assist Maker: Memphis Depay Assists: 13 Team: Olympique Lyonnais

Most Clean Sheets: Alphonse Areola Clean Sheets: 17 Team: Paris Saint-Germain F.C.

Ligue 1 boast some of the most exciting and expensive young talent in world football such as world record transfer fee Neymar Jr, $263 million, and 2018 World Cup winner Kylian Mbappe. The league's most decorated club is Saint-Etienne winning a total of 10 league titles most of which came during the 1960-1970's. Backed by the financial strength of Qatar Sports Investments, Paris Saint-Germain F.C.look to continue their domestic dominance with one of the most expensively assembled teams; squad value $971 million.

GERMANY

Bundesliga

Total Goal Scorer: Robert Lewandowski Goals: 29 Team: FB Bayern Munich

Top Assist Maker: Thomas Muller Assists: 14 Team: Bayern Munich

Most Clean Sheets: Roman Burki, Ralf Fahrmann, Sven Ulreich, Ron-Robert Zieler Clean Sheets: 12 Team: Borussia Dortmund, Schalke 04, Bayern Munich and VfB Stuttgart

Bundesliga is known for its dedicated fan base and high attendance rates. Nestled in tradition, some of the leagues most successful clubs are fan-owned establishments and pride themselves on their connection to civic life in their respective cities. The league's most decorated club is Bayern Munich winning a total of 27 league titles and 7 of the last 10. Red Bull owned RB Leipzig seek to upset the establishment in dethroning, and finacially challenging, Bayern Munich by competing for some of Germany's most promising young talent.

ITALY

Serie A

Top Goal Scorer: Mauro Icardi and Ciro Immobile Goals: 29 Team: Internazionale Milano and Societa Sportiva Lazio S.p.A

Top Assist Maker: Luis Alberto Assists: 14 Team: Societa Sportiva Lazio S.p.A

Most Clean Sheets: Pepe Reina Clean Sheets: 18 Team: Societa Sportiva Calcio Napoli

Serie A has produced some of the most defensively sound teams in recent history. The league is recognized as being the most tactical of the big five European leagues; most games end in 1-0 results. The leagues most decorated club is Juventus F.C. winning a staggering 34 league titles and collecting 7 consecutive titles in their most recent era of dominance. Juventus F.C reign almost came to an end under Maurizio Sarri S.S.C. Napoli. However, Sarri is no longer at the helm and Juventus F.C. might see this as a pivotal season in reestablishing their authority on the league.

SPAIN

La Liga

Top Goal Scorer: Lionel Messi Goals: 34 Team: FC Barcelona

Top Assist Maker: Pablo Fornals, Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez Assists: Team: Villareal and Barcelona

Most Clean Sheets: Jan Oblak Clean Sheets: 22 Team: Atletico Madrid

La Liga is home to some of the most fiery rivalries in club football with derby matches constantly being bringing their respective cities to a screeching halt. No Spanish match draws more attention than El Clasico between European juggernauts Real Madrid and Barcelona. The teams have met 176 times in league play with Real Madrid edging out Barcelona in a 72-to-70 wins record. The most decorated club is Real Madrid boasting a grand total of 33 league titles over their nearest competitors 25 titles. Although, Real Madrid has excelled in European competition in recent years the league has belonged to their hated rivals Barcelona. Prompting the stage for another riveting El Clasico.
